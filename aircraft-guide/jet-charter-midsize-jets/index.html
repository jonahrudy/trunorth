Super midsize jets combine extended range, increased speed, and spacious cabins—making them ideal for cross-country and even transcontinental travel. Frequently Asked Questions About Midsize Jet Charter

What's the difference between midsize jets and light jets?

Midsize jets offer more cabin space, longer range (up to 3,000 nautical miles), and enhanced amenities compared to light jets. They're ideal for coast-to-coast non-stop flights, larger groups of 7-9 passengers, and trips requiring stand-up cabins with enclosed lavatories. Midsize jets provide a better experience for longer flights while remaining more cost-effective than heavy jets.

How many passengers can a midsize jet accommodate?

Midsize jets typically seat 7-9 passengers in spacious, comfortable cabins, some with full stand-up headroom. They feature different seating configurations, enclosed lavatories, accessible galleys, and higher baggage capacity for extended trips.

Can midsize jets fly non-stop coast-to-coast?

Yes! Many midsize jets are designed for non-stop coast-to-coast travel. Routes like Los Angeles to New York, San Francisco to Miami, or Seattle to Boston are easily accomplished without fuel stops, making them ideal for efficient cross-country business travel.

What amenities are standard in midsize jets?

Some midsize jets feature full stand-up cabins while all feature enclosed lavatories and galleys with refreshment service. Many include Wi-Fi connectivity, entertainment systems, conference seating, and more luggage capacity than light jets.

What's the typical range of a midsize jet?

Midsize jets have a range of 2,500-3,500 nautical miles, enabling non-stop flights across the continental United States and to many international destinations including Mexico, Central America, and the Caribbean without fuel stops.

Are midsize jets good for international travel?

Absolutely! Midsize jets excel at international travel within their range. They can easily reach destinations in Mexico, Central America, the Caribbean, and parts of South America non-stop from California. Their enhanced cabin comfort makes longer flights more enjoyable for passengers.
